About

I am a physics graduate in the Zochowski lab at the Univeristy of Michigan. My research primarily focuses on measuring the functional relationships between neurons and studying how these functional networks evolve in time. The goal of my research is to understand the how changes in functional connectivity achieves underlying neuronal function.

Interests

My interests in include network-level encoding, cortex algorithms, the bio-physical mechanisms of neuronal algorithms, and golf... golf is great! Recently I've been looking for systems neuroscience insight from machine learning and information theory.
